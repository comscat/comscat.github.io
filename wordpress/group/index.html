Open-positions​

A Ph.D. scholarship is available for an outstanding student who is eager to apply computational methods to tackle problems related to functional materials and catalysis. The candidates should possess the following qualifications.

  • Achieved Bachelor or Master degree in Chemistry, Physics, or Chemical Engineering 
  • GPA > 3.00, good English communication skills 
  • Interested in using computational approaches, such as DFT, to tackle research problems

The successful candidate will have to enroll a Ph.D. program at the School of Chemistry, Suranaree University of Technology and will be offered

  • Full tuition coverage
  • Stipend varied from 8,000 – 10,000 Baht / month
  • Self-improvement allowance 5,000 Baht / year

If you are excited about joining the COMSCAT group and eager to learn new things, feel free to contact us or send your CV to suthirak@sut.ac.th.
